  <p> 
    It's been a long journey for me. Coming to the US to study was a tough decision for me as an international student. I first studied at Chaffey College, and my goal was to transfer to a four-year university. Getting used to the culture and environment took me almost a year. I thought the journey at Chaffey would only take me less than two years, but it actually took me entirely four years. I met different people and professors during the four year period, and I worked as a math tutor at our college tutoring center for two years before I transferred.I never thought I could get into UC Berkeley since it is my dream school. I was surprised to get the offer but meanwhile felt proud. During the two years at Berkeley, I met some of the smartest people and the best researchers. My mom always asks me if I regretted coming here, and I always tell her I do not regret coming here. I think this is still my best journey so far, and the best is yet to come.
