### Awesome in-class Projects

#### BYOW (Build Your Own World) (JAVA)
It was the final project of CS61B Spring 2019, full description is [here](https://sp19.datastructur.es/materials/proj/proj3/proj3).

Course staff provided the rendering engine, but beside that we needed to figure out how to build the game and what we need. I enjoyed building this little game with my teammate, and we were pretty happy about it.

Project link: [Here](https://github.com/rexhanh/BYOW)
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rexhanh/BYOW/main/assets/demo/ingame0.png" style="width:100%">

#### Maze Runner (Python/ROS)
This was a 4 person final project for my robotics class EECS 106A, full description is [here](https://ucb-ee106.github.io/ee106a-fa19/assets/proj/final_project_guidelines.pdf).\
Our team used Turtle bots for the final project. I am mostly developing the visual/camera part with one of our teammate. For the visual part, we used a Logitech camera and openCV to recognize the green sheet and transform the maze into an array of 0 and 1, 0 for hallways and 1 for the walls.

Project website: [Here](https://robot-gang.github.io/maze-runner/)

#### Dungeon Master (Swift)
I made this little game back in May 2020 when I just started learning Swift. I was pretty proud of it when I finished it. This is basically a RPG game where the player is supposed to kill all the ghosts in four dungeons and face off the final boss after.



#### Covid Information (Swift)
I built this app during the summer of 2020. I was trying to provide a clear covid infection data of the united states, and global total data as well. I was not able to publish this one due to policies in the app store.

Project link: [Here](https://github.com/rexhanh/Covid_info)
